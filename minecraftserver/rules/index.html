Minecraft Server Rules

1) Do not use glitches on this Server! ▶
Glitch exploitation is forbidden.

Warnings and Punishments

MINOR: 1 warn-point (expires in 1 week)

MAJOR: 3 warn-points (expires in 1 month)

CRITICAL: 5 warn-points (expires in 1 year)

Punishments:

  • 3 points: Temp-ban 3 days
  • 5 points: Temp-ban 2 weeks, Discord timeout 3 days
  • 6 points: Temp-ban 3 weeks, Discord timeout 2 weeks
  • 7 points: Permanent ban

By participating and joining this server, you have read and agreed to these rules.
