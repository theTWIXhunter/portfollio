Punishment system:

Warnings and Punishments

On Discord moderation is handled by @Wick. Wick will automatically kick, timeout, or ban users who break the rules. Appeals for Wick's auto-mod actions can be made by following the DM message from Wick.


In Minecraft, use /report <playername> <reason> to report rule violations.

MINOR: 1 warn-point (expires in 1 week)

MAJOR: 3 warn-points (expires in 1 month)

CRITICAL: 5 warn-points (expires in 1 year)

Punishments based on warning points:

  • 3 points: Temp-ban 3 days
  • 5 points: Temp-ban 2 weeks, Discord timeout 3 days
  • 6 points: Temp-ban 3 weeks, Discord timeout 2 weeks
  • 7 points: Permanent ban
